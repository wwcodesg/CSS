WWCode Singapore Hackathon 2018

THEME: DIVERSITY

GOAL:

To create a solutions to promote diversity, or solve an issue related to diversity, whether in society, education or workplace.

27th July -29th July

Venue:

Lifelong Learning Institute

11 Eunos Rd 8, Singapore 408601

Program:

Friday - Training Room, Level 9/1-2-3

  • 6:30pm - Registration
  • 7:00pm - Introduction & Hackathon Briefing
  • 7:30pm -
    • Track 1: Brainstorming - mentors will be available to guide and provide advice.
    • Track 2: Team forming pitch platform
  • 8:00pm - Team registrations
  • 8:30pm - Wrap up

Saturday - Training Room, Level 4/1-2-3-4

  • 8:30am - Breakfast
  • 9:00am - Hackathon start
  • 12:00 noon - Lunch
  • 6:00pm - Dinner
  • 10:00pm - Snack

Sunday

  • 8:30am - Breakfast
  • 12:00 noon - Submission Deadline
  • 12:05 pm - Lunch

Sunday - Lecture Theatre, Level 2

  • 1:30pm - Pitches
  • 2:30pm - Judges deliberation, Event highlights.
  • 3:00pm - Results
  • 3:30pm - Wrap up

Rules:

1. Working prototype is required. A demo must be presented during the pitch.

2. Team size must be 3-9 people. Teams must be registered by 8:30pm on Friday, 27th July.

Categories:

  • Workplace
  • Education
  • Technology

Bonus:

1. Diversity in teams. E.g. Women coders should be well represented (50%) in teams to earn this bonus points.

Prizes:

  • Winning team per category will receive $500 cash/cash vouchers, and 3x50% off voucher for ICAgile Certified Agile Fundamentals Workshop
  • $200 cash/cash vouchers for Runner up per category
  • Bonus: Overall winning team will receive an Oculus Go Virtual Reality headset
  • All participants will receive a goody bag filled with SWAGs from our sponsors.
